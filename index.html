About Kavanagh’s Retreat

Located in the scenic village of Inniskeen, across from the Patrick Kavanagh Centre, this retreat offers guests an immersive experience into the world of the celebrated poet. Whether you’re here for literary inspiration, nature, or a unique glamping experience, Kavanagh’s Retreat has it all.

Location

Kavanagh’s Retreat is conveniently situated:

  • 17 km from Carrickmacross
  • 11 km from Dundalk, County Louth
  • 5 km from Crossmaglen, County Armagh
  • 120 km northwest of Dublin
  • 120 km southwest of Belfast

Explore Activities

Discover the rich experiences awaiting you at Kavanagh's Retreat:

  • Hiking Trails: Explore the scenic trails of Inniskeen, offering stunning views of the countryside and the chance to immerse yourself in nature.
  • Fishing: Enjoy fishing in nearby lakes and rivers, where you can unwind and connect with nature.
  • Cultural Events: Attend local festivals celebrating literature, arts, and the legacy of Patrick Kavanagh.
  • Kavanagh Centre: Visit the Patrick Kavanagh Centre, which offers exhibitions and events that showcase the life and work of the famous poet.
